I am a senior business analytics and information systems major, also pursuing a creative writing certificate from the Magid Center through the publishing track at the University of Iowa.

My writing has been published in multiple student publications, including Horizon and Sanct Literary Magazine. I have also been an art editor for the first issue of Zenith Magazine, and a section Editor for a local student Anthology. Currently, I am an editor for the 6th annual Iowa Chapbook Prize.

Aside from my creative feats, I have also worked on various group projects utilizing R Studio, SQL, and Python. Most recently, my teams have generated an in-depth analysis on features of houses in Ames which have the largest impacts on price, and I am currently working with a health insurance company to pinpoint consumer features which have a lasting impact on health in order to match them with the most beneficial insurance claim.
